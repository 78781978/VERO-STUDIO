Common questions

FAQ

How long does a project take?+

Depending on scope — an image website usually takes 2–3 weeks, while an eCommerce store or AI automation implementation takes 4 to 8 weeks.

Does the price include hosting and a domain?+

Prices cover design and implementation. Hosting and a domain can be purchased separately or connected to an existing client account — we agree on this individually.

Can I order just one pillar, e.g. only a chatbot?+

Yes — each of the four pillars (websites, eCommerce, AI automation, chatbots & agents) can be ordered independently or in any combination.

What happens after the project is finished?+

You receive a post-launch support package, and further development of the website, store or automation is agreed as a separate, flexible collaboration.
