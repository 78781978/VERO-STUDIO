Meet Vero Studio

Boutique approach, enterprise-grade execution

Vero Studio is the brand of Wioletta Pazdańska — a place where brand strategy, precise design and intelligent automation meet in a single, coherent project. We work with brands that want to look and operate premium — from solo businesses, through growing companies, to eCommerce stores.
