Demo 02 · AI Agent

Work that happens in the background

An AI agent is more than a chatbot — it independently handles multi-step tasks: analyzing a request, filling in missing data, preparing a response, and passing the case along. The panel above shows a simplified, looping preview of such a workflow.

  • Multi-step tasks
    The agent combines several steps into one coherent process without manual intervention.
  • Background operation
    It works whether you're online or not.
  • Full control
    You define when the agent acts on its own and when it asks for confirmation.
